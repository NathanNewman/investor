About Investor

This is a competitive investment website aimed at honing in and proving stock market investing skills. This site is great for beginners who wish to practice investing without risking any real money and professionals who are looking to improve their resume. It can also be a fun tool for hobbyists.

After creating an account, the next step is to create a stock portfolio. For each portfolio, users are given $10,000 to buy stocks. Each portfolio must have a minimum of 5 different stocks. Once a portfolio is created, users can continue to buy and sell stocks from the portfolio. The portfolios with the best profit margin are listed on the leaderboard.
